The accent often gives it away immediately. I’m a proud New Zealander (a kiwi!), who has traded the stunning landscapes of home for the bustle of university life in the United States.

I’m currently in the trenches studying Electrical Engineering, driven by a passionate to use technology to solve sustainability problems.

## Why this blog?

I’ve decided to start writing a blog to share my journey, including:
- My research into sustainability tech and environmental problems that I think are interesting
- Opinions and thoughts on Green Tech
- My perspective as a New Zealander living in the US

I've been meaning to build a personal site for a while. My goal is simple: to create a quiet place on the web to share what I build and learn. The truth is, I really don’t know what this blog will look like or eventually become, but I’m excited to find out.

Thanks for reading, and I hope you stick around for more!
